Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pair my wireless device?

Turn on your device and press the pairing button. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the connection.

Where can I find my product serial number?

The serial number is typically located on the bottom of your device or on the product packaging.

How do I update firmware?

Download the latest software from our website and follow the update instructions provided.

What is the warranty period?

Most Logitech products come with a 2-year limited hardware warranty. Check your product page for details.

How do I register my product?

Visit our product registration page and enter your device information to register your warranty.

Can I return my product?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for products purchased directly from Logitech.

Still need help?

Our support team is ready to assist you with any questions or concerns.
