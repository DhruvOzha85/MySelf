We recommend servicing your Honda every 10,000 km or 6 months, whichever comes first.

Yes, we offer complimentary pickup and drop service for customers within city limits.

Can I use third-party parts?

We strongly recommend using genuine Honda parts to maintain warranty and optimal performance.
