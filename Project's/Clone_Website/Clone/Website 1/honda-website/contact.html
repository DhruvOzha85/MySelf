Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I book a test drive?

You can book a test drive online through our website, call our toll-free number, or visit your nearest Honda dealership.

What is the warranty period for Honda cars?

Honda offers a standard warranty of 3 years or 1,00,000 km, whichever comes first. Extended warranty options are also available.

How do I locate my nearest service center?

Use our dealer locator tool on the website or call our customer care number to find the nearest authorized service center.

What financing options are available?

Honda offers attractive financing options through partner banks with competitive interest rates. Contact your dealer for details.

Can I exchange my old car?

Yes, we accept trade-ins. Visit our AutoTerrace section for certified pre-owned cars or contact your dealer for exchange details.
