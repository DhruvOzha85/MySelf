Our Commitment to the Planet

At Logitech, we believe in creating products that enhance people's lives while protecting our planet. Our sustainability journey is focused on reducing our environmental impact through innovative design, responsible sourcing, and circular economy principles.

We're committed to achieving carbon neutrality across our entire product portfolio and operations. From using recycled materials in our products to eliminating plastic from packaging, every decision we make considers the environmental impact.

Join us on our mission to create a more sustainable future through thoughtful design and responsible manufacturing.
