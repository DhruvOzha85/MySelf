Everything you need to know about The Farmer's Dog

Getting Started

How does The Farmer's Dog work?

The Farmer's Dog makes and delivers fresh, healthy dog food right to your door. You start by telling us about your dog, and we create a personalized meal plan based on their specific needs. Your pre-portioned meals arrive fresh, on your schedule, and you simply open and serve.

How much does it cost?

The cost depends on your dog's size, age, activity level, and any health considerations. Prices typically range from $2-12 per day. We believe fresh, healthy food is an investment in your dog's long-term health and happiness.

Can I customize my plan?

Absolutely! You can adjust recipes, delivery frequency, and portion sizes at any time. We make it easy to pause, skip, or cancel shipments whenever you need to.

About Our Food

What's in the food?

Our recipes include USDA-certified proteins (beef, turkey, chicken, or pork), fresh vegetables, healthy grains, and essential vitamins and minerals. Every ingredient meets human food safety standards.

Is the food complete and balanced?

Yes! Every recipe is formulated by board-certified veterinary nutritionists and meets AAFCO standards for complete and balanced nutrition for all life stages.

How should I store the food?

Our food arrives frozen and should be kept in the freezer until you're ready to use it. Once thawed, keep it in the refrigerator and use within 4 days.

Do you offer grain-free options?

We don't currently offer grain-free recipes. Recent studies have linked grain-free diets to heart disease in dogs, and our veterinary nutritionists recommend including healthy whole grains in most dogs' diets.

Delivery & Shipping

How often will I receive deliveries?

You choose your delivery frequency! We recommend deliveries every 2-4 weeks, but you can adjust this at any time based on your needs.

Do you ship to my area?

We ship to all 48 contiguous United States. Unfortunately, we don't currently ship to Alaska, Hawaii, or internationally.

Is shipping free?

Yes! Shipping is always free on all orders, no matter how big or small.

Managing Your Account

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can pause or cancel at any time with no fees or penalties. Just log into your account or contact our customer service team.

How do I contact customer service?

Our customer service team is available 24/7 via email at help@thefarmersdog.com or by phone at (877) 141-8444. We're always here to help!

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! If your dog doesn't love our food, we'll work with you to find a recipe they'll enjoy, or provide a full refund on your first order.
